Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I have my air conditioning system serviced?

It is recommended to have your air conditioning system serviced at least once a year to ensure optimal performance.

2. What are the signs that my air conditioning system needs repair?

Common signs include unusual noises, poor airflow, and inconsistent temperatures. If you notice any of these, contact us for a check-up.

3. How can I improve the efficiency of my air conditioning system?

Regular maintenance, changing filters, and ensuring proper insulation can significantly improve efficiency.

4. What is the average lifespan of an air conditioning unit?

With proper maintenance, an air conditioning unit can last between 10 to 15 years.

5. Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency services for urgent air conditioning issues.
